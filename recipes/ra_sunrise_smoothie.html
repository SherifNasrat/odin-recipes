Ra's Sunrise Smoothie

Description

A refreshing and healthy smoothie that pays homage to Ra, the ancient Egyptian god of the sun.

Ingredients

  • a Banana
  • 1/2 cup of chopped Mango
  • 1/2 cup of pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup ofunsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon of chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • a pinch of ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Blend the banana, mango, pineapple, almond milk, and honey until smooth.
  2. Add the chia seeds and ground cinnamon, and blend again until everything is well mixed.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy it chilled.
