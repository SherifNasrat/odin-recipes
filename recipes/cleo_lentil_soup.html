Cleopatra's Lentil Soup

Description

A hearty soup that was inspired by Cleopatra, the famous Pharaoh of Egypt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of red lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 6 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of coriander
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook for 5 minutes or until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the red lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, coriander, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30 minutes, or until the lentils are cooked through.
  5. Serve the soup hot with a slice of bread.
