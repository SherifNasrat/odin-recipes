Tutankhamun's Spiced Chicken

Description

A flavorful and easy-to-make chicken dish that's fit for a Pharaoh.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, cumin, paprika, cinnamon, coriander, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the chicken breasts, making sure they're coated evenly.
  4. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through.
  5. Serve the chicken hot with lemon wedges on the side.
